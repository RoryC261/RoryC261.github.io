Yearly AGM

Hey guys, it's almost time for the AGM of Gaming Society, this is where we hand over to a new society (or keep the same, just depends how it all goes). This is one of the most important events for gaming and requires as much people to be there as possible from the society. The AGM will be held on the 25th of March during the first 30 minutes of gaming society, so even if you just come along for the AGM and leave afterwards we would appreciate it a lot! If you are coming, click on "going" so we can keep count on how many people will come.
